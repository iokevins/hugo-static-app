From my supervisor, Dan:  
  

*   Wooden toothpick, frayed end, to apply solder paste
*   Heat gun at 630 degrees
*   Touch hand poke
*   Tacky flux
*   Paste solder
*   SMD stands for Solder Mask Defined
*   Tip tinner
