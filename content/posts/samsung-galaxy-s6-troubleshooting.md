FORCE REBOOT  
Volume down and Power/Lock keys, for 10–20 seconds  
  
RECOVERY MODE  
From power off: Power Button, Volume Up Button and the Home Buttons at the Same Time  

  
FORCE POWER OFF  
Plug-in to charger, then as per Force Reboot...it will power off
